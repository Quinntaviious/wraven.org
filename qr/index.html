QR Safety Tips

Check the domain. Tap-and-hold to preview the full URL. Watch for typos and weird subdomains.
Look for HTTPS + known brand. Don't sign in on sites you don't recognize.
Avoid installing apps from QR codes. Go to the official app store instead.
When in doubt, don't submit. Close the page; find the destination manually.

What is WRAVEN?

WRAVEN (Western Research Advisory for Vulnerabilities, Exploits, & Networks) is Western Michigan University's premier cybersecurity nonprofit and student organization. We teach ethical hacking & defending, run workshops and labs, compete as ECX Inc., publish research, and collaborate with nonprofits and small businesses to strengthen our community's security.
