🛡️ WRAVEN's Mission at WMU

WRAVEN (Western Research Advisory for Vulnerabilities Exploits & Networks) is Western Michigan University's premier cybersecurity registered student organization. We're WMU's leading cybersecurity club & nonprofit, dedicated to advancing cybersecurity knowledge through hands-on research, competitive CTF participation, and real-world threat analysis at Western Michigan University.

🎯 What WRAVEN Does at Western Michigan University

  • Cybersecurity Threat Intelligence Research: We analyze emerging cybersecurity threats and develop countermeasures for the WMU community
  • WMU CTF Competitions: Our team ECX Inc. represents Western Michigan University in international Capture The Flag cybersecurity events
  • Vulnerability Research at WMU: We discover and responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities to protect WMU and beyond
  • WMU Cybersecurity Community Education: We share cybersecurity knowledge through workshops, presentations, and open-source tools for WMU students

🏛️ Academic Excellence at Western Michigan University

Founded in partnership with Western Michigan University's Computer Club, WRAVEN bridges the gap between academic cybersecurity education and real-world security challenges. Our WMU members gain practical cybersecurity experience that complements their formal education at Western Michigan University.
