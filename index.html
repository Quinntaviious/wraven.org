What is WRAVEN?

×

🛡️ Our Mission

WRAVEN (Western Research Advisory for Vulnerabilities Exploits & Networks) is Western Michigan University's premier cybersecurity registered student organization. We're dedicated to advancing cybersecurity knowledge through hands-on research, competitive CTF participation, and real-world threat analysis.

🎯 What We Do

  • Threat Intelligence Research: We analyze emerging cybersecurity threats and develop countermeasures
  • CTF Competitions: Our team ECX Inc. competes internationally in Capture The Flag events
  • Vulnerability Research: We discover and responsibly disclose security vulnerabilities
  • Community Education: We share knowledge through workshops, presentations, and open-source tools

🏛️ Academic Excellence

Founded in partnership with Western Michigan University's Computer Club, WRAVEN bridges the gap between academic cybersecurity education and real-world security challenges. Our members gain practical experience that complements their formal education.
