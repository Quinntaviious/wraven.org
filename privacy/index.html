Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Overview

WRAVEN (Western Research Advisory for Vulnerabilities, Exploits, & Networks) is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about our data practices. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain technical information:

  • IP Address: For security monitoring and analytics
  • Browser Type & Version: To ensure compatibility
  • Device Information: Basic device and screen information
  • Access Logs: Timestamps and pages visited
  • Referrer Information: Where you came from (when available)

Information You Provide

We only collect information you voluntarily provide:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email, and message content
  • Research Submissions: Academic or professional inquiries
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Email address for updates

How We Use Information

Primary Uses

  • Security: Monitor for threats and protect our infrastructure
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide updates
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve our services
  • Research: Aggregate data for cybersecurity research (anonymized)

We Do NOT

  • Sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • Use tracking cookies for advertising purposes
  • Store sensitive personal information unnecessarily
  • Profile users for commercial purposes

Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

As a cybersecurity organization, we implement industry-standard security practices:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and updates
  • Incident Response: Established procedures for security incidents

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary:

  • Contact Information: Until you request removal
  • Access Logs: 90 days for security purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized after 1 year

Third-Party Services

External Links

Our website contains links to external services (GitHub, Discord, etc.). These services have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

Hosted Services

Our website may use minimal third-party services:

  • Fonts: Google Fonts (cached locally when possible)
  • Analytics: Self-hosted analytics tools (no external tracking)
  • CDN: Content delivery networks for performance

Your Rights

Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

International Users

We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other regional regulations.

Cookies & Local Storage

Essential Cookies Only

We use minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Session Cookies: For basic site functionality
  • Preferences: Remember your settings (theme, language)
  • Security: Protect against CSRF and other attacks

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Academic & Research Use

Educational Context

As a university-affiliated organization, some activities may involve:

  • Research Publications: Anonymized data in academic papers
  • Student Projects: Data used for educational purposes
  • Cybersecurity Research: Threat intelligence and security analysis

All research use follows ethical guidelines and institutional review processes.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may provide additional notice through our website or email communications.
